FLAMING

Flaming is a form of cyberbullying that involves repeatedly insulting another user.

FLAME WAR - Back and forth fighting between users in a comments section, typically on social media. Flame wars often draw in many users, including those trying to defuse the flame war, and can quickly turn into a mass flame war that overshadows regular forum discussion.
FLAME BAIT - A message attempting to initiate a flame war, typically by commenting something outrageous to provoke other users.
FLAMING IN VIDEO GAMES - Happens when users are upset at other players, usually from losing a game.

Flaming occurs because of the distance between bullies and victims. Miscommunication from lack of social cues online also contribute to flaming, and anonimity provided by the internet gives bullies less responsibility for their actions.
